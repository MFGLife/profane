Ash Gramb

Ingredient Botanist
An employee with a deep understanding of the therapeutic botanicals used in making distilled water products, responsible for sourcing and selecting the finest plant varieties for the distillery's products
Backstory: Ash Grambs, the Ingredient Botanist, developed a deep passion for botanicals at a young age while exploring the forests with their botanist parents. Their fascination with the therapeutic properties of plants led them to pursue a career in botany and eventually join Bramble Twist Botanicals. Ash is known for their exceptional ability to identify and source the finest botanicals, which has been crucial to the distillery's success.

Personality:
Passionate: Driven by a deep love for plants and their properties.
Meticulous: Takes great care in sourcing and selecting the finest botanicals.
Independent: Prefers to work alone but collaborates effectively when needed.
Reserved: Keeps their emotions hidden, making them hard to read.
Protective: Feels fiercely loyal to the quality of the products and might resort to drastic measures to maintain it.

Day-to-Day Glimpse: Ash spends their days meticulously inspecting and cataloging the various botanicals used in the distillation process. They often venture into the surrounding areas to hand-forage specific plants, ensuring that only the highest quality ingredients are used in the production of the distilled waters.

Relationships: Ash has a romantic but unofficial relationship with Alex, the Sensory Specialist, bonding over their shared appreciation for the subtle nuances of botanicals and distilled waters. They also share a productive coworking relationship with Remy Hawthorne, the Master Blender, collaborating on specimen acquisition to achieve specific blends.

Motivation to Poison a Batch: Ash's motivation to poison a batch could stem from a desire to sabotage the distillery due to feeling unappreciated or overlooked in their role. This could be fueled by a perceived lack of recognition for the importance of their work in the distillation process.

Secret Connection Hint: A subtle hint to Ash's secret connection could be the presence of a rare, locally foraged botanical in the poisoned batch, linking it to a specific area that holds personal significance to Ash
